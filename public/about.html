Our Story

Founded in 2018, AMIGO was born from a singular vision: to redefine modern luxury menswear by blending timeless European craftsmanship with contemporary design sensibility.

Every garment we create is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, sustainability, and the belief that true luxury lies in the details.

Our Values

Exceptional Quality

We source the finest materials from Italy, Scotland, and Japan — from cashmere and merino wool to hand-finished leathers. Each piece undergoes rigorous quality control.

Sustainable Craft

Sustainability is woven into everything we do. Our supply chain is transparent, our packaging is eco-conscious, and we partner with artisans who share our environmental commitment.

Client First

From our VIP concierge service to complimentary alterations, we believe luxury extends beyond the garment. Our clients are partners in a lifelong sartorial journey.

The AMIGO Atelier

Where Vision Meets Craftsmanship

Our atelier in Milan is where every AMIGO piece comes to life. A team of 40 master tailors, each with over 15 years of experience, hand-finishes every garment with meticulous attention to detail.

From the initial sketch to the final stitch, every step is guided by our founding principle: that modern men deserve clothing that speaks to both their ambition and their taste.
